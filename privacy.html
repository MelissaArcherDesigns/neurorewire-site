Privacy Policy

Effective date: to be set at first release.

NeuroRewire is built so that we cannot see your data. This policy is short because there is almost nothing to disclose.

What we collect

Nothing. NeuroRewire has no accounts, no server, no analytics, no advertising, no crash reporting, and no third-party software that connects to the internet. The app never transmits your information anywhere.

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — your focuses, the “old tapes” you write down, your affirmations, journal entries, intensity ratings, streaks, and settings — is stored in a single file on your device only, protected by your device’s built-in encryption. We have no copy and no way to access it.

App lock

If you enable the app lock, authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint, passcode, PIN, or pattern) is handled entirely by your device’s operating system. NeuroRewire never sees or stores your biometric data — the OS only tells the app “unlocked” or “not unlocked.”

Notifications

If you enable a daily reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device. No notification service of ours is involved, because we don’t have one.

Export and backup

You can export your data as a file at any time. That file is yours: you choose where it goes (e.g., your own iCloud Drive, Google Drive, or local storage). Anything you do with an exported file is under your control and outside the app.

Data deletion

Delete the app and your data is gone (along with any device backups governed by your OS settings). There is nothing to request from us, because we hold nothing.

Children

NeuroRewire is not directed at children under 13 and collects no data from anyone, including children.

This website

This website is a static page. It sets no cookies, runs no scripts, and uses no analytics or trackers.

Changes

If a future version ever changes any of the above (for example, adding an optional sync feature), this policy will be updated first and the change will be clearly announced in the app before it takes effect.

Contact

A contact email will be published here before the app’s release.
