A daily practice, a few quiet minutes

The old tape isn’t a fact. It’s a recording.

Old criticisms replay because your brain predicts from old data. NeuroRewire is a short daily ritual: settle your system, contradict the old line out loud, and stack up evidence — so the tape stores back a little weaker each time.

Coming soon to the App Store Free. No account. Nothing leaves your phone.

One session, five quiet steps

Designed to be done daily — one session is a thought, thirty is a pathway.

1 · Choose

Pick your focus

Four built-in themes, or build your own from a specific voice, memory, or trigger.

2 · Arrive

Breathe first

Six slow rounds with a glowing orb. Long exhales tell your nervous system the threat is over.

3 · Rewire

Say the new line

Face the old tape, speak your replacement out loud, and lock it in with slow left-right eye movement or tapping.

4 · Evidence

Log the proof

One line against each old belief. Every entry you re-read later weakens the automatic story.

5 · Done

Watch it stick

Streaks, your proof archive, and charts of the old tape getting quieter week over week.

Private by architecture, not by promise

What you write in NeuroRewire is nobody else’s business — so the app is built to make collection impossible, not just avoided.

Nothing collected

No account, no analytics, no ads, no crash reporting. The app makes no network connections at all.

Everything on-device

Your entries live in one file on your phone, protected by your device’s built-in encryption.

Lock it down

Optional Face ID / passcode lock, and your journal is hidden from the app switcher.

Yours to keep

Export your data as a plain file anytime. Restore it on any device — the file belongs to you.
