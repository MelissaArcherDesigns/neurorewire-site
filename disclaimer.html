Important Disclaimer

Shown and acknowledged inside the app during onboarding; always reachable in Settings.

NeuroRewire is a self-reflection and wellness tool. It is not therapy, medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

  • The exercises in this app — breathing, affirmations, journaling, and bilateral stimulation — are practices for everyday negative self-talk: the nagging inner voice, the cringe loop, the old criticism on repeat.
  • The bilateral stimulation in this app is inspired by techniques used in EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing), but it is not EMDR and is not a substitute for working with a licensed, EMDR-trained therapist.
  • If a memory brings up real distress when you touch it — panic, flashbacks, feeling unsafe — stop. That is territory for a trained professional, and working with one goes a level deeper than any app can.
  • NeuroRewire does not treat, cure, or prevent any medical or psychological condition, and nothing in the app should be read as a claim that it does.
  • Never disregard professional medical or mental-health advice, or delay seeking it, because of something in this app.

If you are in crisis: contact your local emergency services. In the United States, call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) — available 24/7. Outside the US, find local helplines at findahelpline.com.

By using NeuroRewire, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this disclaimer.
